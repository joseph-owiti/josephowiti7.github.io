ABOUT ME

History

I am Joseph Paul Owiti and I was born on January 10, 1998. For the longest time, my home was near a western city in Kenya called Kisumu. My specific home town is called Muhoroni, nearing the large Lake Victoria. I have four siblings who are all older than me. Currently, I reside in Virginia as an international student and live in a suburb called Fredericksburg. I am a Junior Computer Science student.

Passions

I want to help people. People we talk to and people we walk past in the streets all have a story, and they have struggles. People are hurting around us and they are all searching for a solution or an answer. So if I can be part of that solution, if I can find a way to help others find their answers, then my purpose is met. Some extra-curricular activities I enjoy are Ultimate Frisbee, Soccer, Volleyball, Lacrosse, and Chinese Food. The world is beautiful and needs to be seen, so I love the outdoors.

Interests

I intend to use my acquired technical skills and software knowledge in any form of Software Development. I love to learn new things and always wholeheartedly pursue what is before me. Any part of Software Development, Web Programming, and Database Design & Programming are three areas that I know I will enjoy.
